About Me

Howdy! I'm an incoming PhD student in Vanderbilt's Quantitative & Chemical Biology program who specializes in turning messy, cross-disciplinary problems into systematic, data-driven solutions. My toolkit was developed in settings that range from processing 100 + HPLC chromatograms in Denmark, where I wrote Python scripts to clean, analyze, and visualize the data, to building a full-stack lipidomics website that will allow real-time insight into lipidomics data and experiments. More recently, I designed reliability tools for a production RAG framework, experience that sharpened my machine-learning instincts and taught me where computational shortcuts break down in the real world. Now I am very excited to take the next step in pursuing graduate research, with longterm goals to progress rational enzyme and peptide design: leveraging ML models to streamline therapeutic discovery.

Analysis of Factors Influencing the Concentration of Bioactive Compounds in Rhodiola Rosea Complete

Worked with a cross cultural team of 4 in Denmark to correlate how the concentrations of rosavin and rosarin are impacted by location of growth and plant sex (as it is a dioecious plant). My primary role involved data analysis and visualization of 100+ HPLC chromatagrams including method validation, ANOVA analysis, and post hoc significance testing.

Qualitative Evaluation of Cancer Treatments on the Cell Cycle with Fluorescence Microscopy Complete

Led a team of 3 to investigate the impact MLN 4924, Hydroxyurea, and an undisclosed compound-X on Fucci system osteosarcoma cancer cells. Used florescent microscopy with the Fuji software to track the expression of fluorophors in the cells over time. The variations in fluorophore production were qualitatively assessed to help narrow down and support proposed mechanisms of action for the assorted anticancer compounds.

  • FUCCI System
  • Cell Culture
  • Fuji Software

The stack is Django + Bootstrap on the front end with a Postgres database running on Amazon RDS, giving the service a clean UI and cloud-hosted reliability.
